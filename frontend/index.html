Understanding Bitcoin

🌐

Decentralized Network

Bitcoin operates on a peer-to-peer network without a central authority. All transactions are verified by network nodes through cryptography and recorded on a public blockchain.

📊

Market Volatility

As the first cryptocurrency, Bitcoin's price is highly dynamic. It's influenced by global adoption, macroeconomic trends, regulatory news, and overall market sentiment.
